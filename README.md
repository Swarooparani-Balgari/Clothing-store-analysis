## Clothing Store Analysis

## Objective
An Online Clothing store wants to analyze sales to understand top-performing categories, discount impact, and city-wise revenue.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- SQL
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI

## Dataset
The Dataset contains the following :
- OrderID
- OrderDate
- City
- Category
- Product
- Quantity
- Price
- DiscountPercent
- CustomerType

## Analysis Performed
- Calculated Discount Amount, Final Price, Without Discount and Total Revenue
- Analyzed revenue by Product
- Compared evenue across cities
- Evaluated category-wise revenue distribution
- Created visualizations for business insights

## Key Insights
- Women category generates the highest revenue among all categories
- Delhi dominates the other cities revenue
- Jeans is the top-performing product among all products
- New customer type dominates the returning customer type revenue
- Customer whose OrderID is 7007 generates the highest revenue among all customers
- On the day 19-03-2026 had the highest revenue than others
- On the whole,"Discounts did not increase sales. Total revenue with discount (14,690) rupees,is lower than revenue without discount (16,700) rupees, indicating discounts reduced overall sales by (2010) Rupees."
